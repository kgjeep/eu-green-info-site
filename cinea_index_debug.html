CINEA in 2 minutes

The European Climate, Infrastructure and Environment Executive Agency (CINEA), officially established on 15 February 2021, started its activities on 1 April 2021 in order to implement parts of certain EU programmes. 

CINEA plays a key role in supporting the European Commission to advance economic development, enhance competitiveness and protect nature.
